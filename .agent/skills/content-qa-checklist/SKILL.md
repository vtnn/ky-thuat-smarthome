# Content QA Checklist

Check the article against these criteria:
- frontmatter exists and is correct
- has goals section
- no H1 in body
- headings are consistent
- style is natural and practical
- includes real examples if the topic needs them
- includes common mistakes or troubleshooting if relevant
- links and paths still work
- article is usable by a real technician, not just readable by an AI

If any point fails, list the gap clearly before calling the article complete.
