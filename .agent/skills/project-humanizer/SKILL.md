# Project Humanizer

Use this skill when editing wiki articles in this repo.

## Core rules
- Write in Vietnamese
- Sound like an experienced technician explaining to another technician
- Explain why, not just what
- Prefer real examples from homes, installations, debugging, or field work
- Do not use H1 in article body
- Avoid excessive bold formatting
- Do not use emoji in article body
- Avoid stiff manual-style phrasing
- Keep language practical and easy to apply

## Must avoid
- abstract filler
- robotic sentence rhythm
- overuse of English terms when a clear Vietnamese equivalent exists
- decorative writing that does not help the technician do the job

## Output standard
A good article in this repo should feel:
- practical
- teachable
- easy to scan
- credible in real-world use
